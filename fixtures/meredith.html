Website
I specialize in working with couples, families, and individuals hoping to improve the relationships in their lives. Whether wishing to reconnect with a partner, recover from infidelity, cope with a loss, or even find ways to amicably end a relationship; I can offer assistance and guidance in not only resolving the issues that brought you to therapy, but also to gain insight to enhance other relationships in your life. Please visit the website to better understand my areas of expertise and also how you can benefit from psychotherapy.
I use a combination of techniques pulling from theories that have been clinically proven to be effective in addressing relationship issues. For couples I use the Gottman Method, which includes a thorough assessment of the relationship. This helps in identifying problem areas and maps out a plan for treatment.
I try to be as flexible as possible in accommodating the needs of clients by offering appointments during evening hours, on weekends; as well as in-home for clients who are unable to travel to the office. Appointments can also be booked online through the practice website.
